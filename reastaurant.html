About the Burger Factory

The Burger Factory is a local, and family owned business for 3 generations. Jenson created the restaurant in 1894, his grandson now took over his fathers roots. Dave created the restaurant in 1920, because he could never find a burger that tasted the right way. They serve some of the best burgers in town. Any burger lover would love the food here. We are famous for our behemoth monster burger challenge
